🔤 Lab L01 — Glossary Kick Start

DueSaturday by 11:59pm
Points100
SubmittingWebsite URL, media recording, or file upload

Objective

Create a mini glossary that encapsulates key concepts and terminologies related to Artificial Intelligence (AI). This exercise aims to familiarize you with the process of researching, understanding, and concisely presenting complex information — and serves as a precursor to the comprehensive AI Glossary you'll develop for your mid-term assignment.

Instructions
1 Select Terms
  • Choose 26 terms — one for each letter of the alphabet — related to AI
  • Ensure terms cover a broad spectrum of concepts to demonstrate depth and understanding
  • Avoid including terms unrelated to AI domains
2 Research
  • For each term, conduct thorough online research
  • Utilize reputable sources such as academic journals, industry publications, and authoritative websites
  • Strive to understand not only the definition but also the context and application of each term in AI
3 Write Definitions
  • Write a concise, clear definition for each term
  • Limit your definition to two or three sentences to ensure clarity and brevity
  • Where applicable, include an example or a real-world application to provide context
4 Create Visuals Optional but Recommended
  • For each term, consider creating or sourcing a visual representation (e.g., diagram, infographic, or symbol)
  • Visuals should be simple, relevant, and aid in the understanding of the term
5 Compile the Glossary
  • Organize the terms alphabetically in a document
  • For each term, include the definition and corresponding visual (if available)
  • Ensure the document is well-formatted, easy to navigate, and visually appealing
6 Cite Your Sources
  • Provide proper citations for your definitions and visuals
  • Adhere to a consistent citation format
  • Include a bibliography at the end of the document listing all sources consulted
7 Review and Refine
  • Proofread your glossary to correct any spelling, grammar, or factual errors
  • Ensure that the information is accurate, definitions are clear, and visuals are relevant and properly credited
8 Submission
  • Submit your practice glossary by the specified deadline
  • Be prepared to receive feedback and use it to improve your research and presentation skills for the mid-term AI Glossary assignment
  • Name your submission as L01_TeamName_SubmitterName_ITAI1370
⚠️ Term Project Notice

This glossary becomes a Term Project that continues throughout the term of the course. Each week, add to the glossary new terms you have learned about, in the appropriate glossary subdivision. Your team will expand your glossary file module by module, and at the end of the term submit the full glossary as a Term Project.

📄 Assignment A01 — The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

DueSaturday by 11:59pm
Points100
SubmittingText entry, URL, media recording, or file upload

Objective

Research and produce a comprehensive paper on the history of artificial intelligence, tracing its development from early concepts to modern advancements.

Required Sections
1.
Introduction
  • Briefly explain what artificial intelligence is
  • State the purpose of your paper and what you aim to cover
2.
Historical Milestones
  • Early Concepts: Research early ideas and theories related to AI (e.g., myths, philosophical works, early machines)
  • Foundational Events: Explore significant events that laid the groundwork for AI (e.g., Alan Turing's work, the Dartmouth Conference of 1956)
  • Technological Advancements: Highlight key developments and breakthroughs (e.g., neural networks, expert systems, machine learning algorithms)
  • Modern Era: Discuss contemporary AI advancements including deep learning, NLP, and autonomous systems
3.
Influential Figures
  • Profile key individuals who have significantly contributed to the development of AI
  • e.g., Alan Turing, John McCarthy, Marvin Minsky, Geoffrey Hinton
4.
Ethical and Societal Impacts
  • Examine the ethical considerations and societal impacts of AI throughout its history
5.
Future Directions
  • Speculate on potential future developments and challenges in the field of AI
6.
Conclusion
  • Summarize the key points discussed in the paper
  • Reflect on the overall significance of AI's evolution
7.
References
  • Cite all sources used in your research following your preferred citation style
Paper Requirements
Length
5–7 pages, double-spaced
Sources
At least 3 peer-reviewed sources
